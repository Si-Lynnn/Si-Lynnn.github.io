Hello there! I am Zilin, a third-year PhD student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University. I am working on robot design and manipulation based on Delta robots and co-advised by Prof. Zeynep Temel and Prof. Oliver Kroemer. Currently I am on a leave of absence and doing a full-time student researcher internship with Google DeepMind. I did 2023 summer research internship with Dr. Chuang Gan at MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab.

I received my Master degree in Robotics from Carnegie Mellon University. I had a great time working in the RoboTouch Lab, supervised by Prof. Wenzhen Yuan. My research was about example-based tactile simulation and its sim-to-real applications.

Prior to my Masters, I completed my Bachelor degree in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University. I worked with Prof. Laurent Kneip in Mobile Perception Lab as an undergraduate researcher; I also worked with Prof. Michael Kaess in Robot Perception Lab as RISS intern.

I enjoy climbing, cuddling cats and dogs, and dancing outside of work.
